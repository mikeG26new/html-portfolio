My Favorite Movies (So far)

My selection for top 3 movies that I could watch again.

Caddy Shack

For some mindless entertainment, you cannot go wrong with Caddy Shack. Many qoutes are instantly reconizable. For example. "It in the hole" or "keep playing through the heavy stuff". You will find someone whom will understand what you are talking about.

The Best Years of our Lives

The film portrayed three men returning home after deployment. Each one of the men has to struggle to adapt to postwar life.

Marty

An underrated gem of movie. Featuring Ernest Borgine whom is older single guy working as a butcher whom recieves a lot of pressure to get married. When he does find his gal, he has to deal with opposition from friends and family.
